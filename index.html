FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is RAG?
RAG stands for Retrieval-Augmented Generation. It combines a search step — finding the most relevant passages in your own documents — with a language model that drafts a clear answer. Because answers are tied to real sources, the output is grounded and verifiable, not made up.
Do answers include citations?
Yes. Every answer links back to the specific document and page where the information was found. You can click through to verify the source before using it in your work.
Can we limit which documents are used?
Absolutely. You can organize documents into collections and restrict queries to specific folders, case files, or document types — so answers always come from the right context.
How is our data handled?
Your documents are encrypted at rest and in transit. We do not use your data to train models. Each workspace is fully isolated, and you retain ownership and control of all uploaded materials.
How long does setup take?
Most firms are up and running within a day. Upload your documents, and Ragbase begins indexing immediately. No engineering resources or complex integrations required.
Does this replace attorneys?
No. Ragbase is a research tool that helps attorneys work faster by surfacing relevant information from their own documents. All legal judgment and decision-making remain with the attorney.
